Computer Science & Engineering

About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was started in the year 2007 with an intake of 60 students. The department has been recognized by AICTE New Delhi, affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum. The department consists of highly qualified and experienced faculty with good recognition in academia and industry. Department provides very good academic learning environment complemented by research work. The department offers B.E. and Ph.D. (full time and part time) degrees with comprehensive curriculum on topics related to all aspects of computer hardware and software.

Vision

To develop competent technical professionals with value based education in the area of Computer Science and Engineering

Mission

Impart quality technical education through dedicated team, relevant curriculum, teaching and research in the area of Computer Science and Engineering
Facilitate and foster effective interactions in a conducive environment for students of the department with different stake holders to achieve professional and personal growth

Achievements

Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Govt SKSJ Technological Institute received fund for Rs 02.00 lakhs (Rupees Two Lakhs only) towards Faculty Development Programme titled “Wireless Mesh Networks: Challenges and Research Directions”. It was successfully conducted during 12th March 2012 and 16th March 2012.
The Department of CSE has got extension of approval for “Research Center” for the year 2013-14. There are one full time research scholar and four part time research scholars registered for Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Dr. T G Basavaraju. To facilitate the research activities, QUALNET network simulator and XATA simulator were purchased through TEQIP –II for the benefit of staff, students and research scholars in September 2013 to carry out research in the area of networking.
Dr T G Basavaraju, Professor and Head, Department of Department of Computer Science and Engineering has nominated as Member in BOS (CSE), VTU Belgaum for the period of 2014-2017 and BOE (CSE), VTU, Belgaum for the period of 2013-14.
Dr T G Basavaraju, Professor and Head, Department of Department of Computer Science and Engineering published a text book on “Computer Networks-I” Elsevier Publishers during September 2012.
Dr. T G Basavaraju has identified as Expert Member by NBA, New Delhi as a evaluator. He has also been invited by several colleges to deliver expert lectures in the area of Computer Networks.
Mr. Pradeep kumar, Associate Professor in CSE Dept is pursuing Ph.D. in JNTU, Kakinada.
Nagashree Bhat, student of fifth semester is nominated as Google Student Ambassador for GSKSJTI.

Infrastructure

The department has state of art infrastructure and good computing environment equipped and supported by high speed LAN. The Department is headed by Dr. T G Basavaraju, Professor in Computer Science and Engineering who has rich experience in teaching and industry. The department consists of two associate professors and six assistant professors. Some of the faculty members are working towards their Ph.D. and have set up research facilities to pursue the same. The students are given good computing and internet facility and created good academic ambience.

Forum

The Computer Science Student forum “DOTSLASH” provides platform to all the students to showcase their hidden talent in co-curricular and extracurricular activities. A technical fest ”IMPULSE” is conducted every year under the CSE forum and a series of technical events will be arranged to benefit the students in overall development. Staff and Students are also encouraged to participate in workshop, conferences and seminars and technical events in other colleges. Experts from industry and academia are invited to deliver talks in the emerging areas of Computer Science and Engineering. Students are also trained in soft and technical skills to prepare them to face the industry with confidence.
